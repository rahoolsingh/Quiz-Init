Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can participate in this Quiz Competition?

    Every student of Sarala Birla University, Ranchi is eligible to join this event.

  • Is there any departments/stream criteria required?

    No, students of any department/stream can participate in this event.

  • Is this competition paid?

    No, this event is completely free. You don't have to pay any amount for this event.

  • Is there any kind of rewards for the winners and perticipants?

    Yes, Winners will be awarded with a valid Certificate from Sarala Birla University from the officials. Every member of team will be awarded with their own Certificate of winning position (i.e., Gold, Silver and Bronze).

    *Participants will also get a Digital "Certificate of Participation".
